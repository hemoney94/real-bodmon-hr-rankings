How the ranking works

The page combines Ballpark Pal matchup probability and hitter park/weather factors with recent Baseball Savant contact quality. Recent form uses barrels, hard-hit rate, 105+ and 110+ mph contact, hard airborne contact, max exit velocity, hits and home runs from the five days before the slate. Average exit velocity is intentionally excluded because weak grounders can hide premium home-run contact. Small samples are pulled toward neutral. Pitcher Attack Score uses the starter’s recent zone location, heart-of-zone and meatball rates, barrels and hard contact allowed, and home runs per 100 plate appearances. Overall Zone% receives limited weight; pitches over the heart matter much more.
