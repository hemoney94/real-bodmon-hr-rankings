How the ranking works

The page pulls all games, all batter-vs-starting-pitcher matchups, hitter park/weather factors, and game projections. The primary score is the matchup HR probability multiplied by the player’s HR park factor. When a matchup probability is unavailable, it estimates 1+ HR probability from projected home runs and then applies the park factor.
